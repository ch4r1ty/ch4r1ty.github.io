从句和介词短语互换：

  1. function as 替换 am/is/are
    同理 serve as/ work as
  2. Simultaneously 替换 at the same time
    同时有关：troublesome/ burdensome/ arduous
  3. It it adj for sb. to do sth. （it 替代的是 to do sth.）
  4. perfect/ better 替换 improve
  5. pedagogical method 替换 teaching method
  6. 表示目的，with the purpose of 替换 to
    In order to do/ with the aim of(注意of!)/ for the sake of doing
  7. monotonous 代替 boring，无聊的
    naive and monotonous
  8. regarding/ concerning 代替 about，关于
  9. distract oneself from 代替 cannot，不会
    distract sb from
  10. Nutritionally-deficient 和 junk food 一样
  11. confer with 代替 discuss with
  12. negotiate with 代替 talk with，和…交流
  13. expose to 代替 face with，状从那边的开头 Though exposed to
  14. sb be placed as the beneficiaries 代替 benefit sb
    place sb as beneficiaries
  15. 常用抽象名词：acceptance/ adoption
  16. 判断强调句是否正确：把 it is 和 that 去掉，还正确的话，那这个强调句就是正确
  17. crave 代替 like
  18. connection/ bond 代替 relationship，友谊
  19. establish relationship 建立关系；maintain relationship 维系关系
  20. intimate and harmonious relationship 亲密和谐的关系
  21. brimmed with 代替 full of，充满，形容多的 (Brimmed with challenges, a journey is …)
  22. undergo/ experience + depression/ frustration 代替 will be sad
  23. undergo insomnia 经历失眠
  24. during the process of doing … 代替 by …
  25. in personal and professional life 代替 in life
  26. render assistance 代替 help，render 指的是要有实际的帮助
  27. demonstrated and presented by sb 代替 showed by sb
  28. daily worries and concerns 代替 daily worries
  29. elements of novelty and freshness 替换 fresh element
  30. proficient in 代替 good at
  31. corporation 代替 company
  32. metropolis 代替 big city
    metropolitan 大城市的
  33. deteriorate citizens’ health 危害了人们的健康
  34. practice/ hone/ develop + skills 磨练技能
    cultivate/ nurture/ foster + personalities 磨练品质
  35. a simple shift in … will occur when …
  36. mass transit 平替 public transportation
  37. reside in 代替 live in
    dwell in
    inhabit in
  38. objective 代替 goal，目标
  39. interact and communicate with 代替 chat with
  40. force success 代替 succeed

换主语的三个方法：

一、变换主语

  1. 非人做主语
  2. Ving 作主语
  3. it is adj (for sb.) to do sth.

二、给主语戴帽子：（这种情况主语基本上都是人）

  1. 介词短语
    With the …, sb will …
  2. 非谓语
    Ving, Ved
    Faced with/ Facing/ Encountering/ Confronting sth
  3. 状语从句的省略
    从句和主句 主语一样的时候，省略主语
    也就是 When/ Because/ After + Ving/ Vet

非谓语（关于句子的尾巴）

原则上，加或不加逗号，意思没有偏差。考试中，都加上逗号！

  1. 对于名词的修饰
    n + Ving/ Ved
    e.g 竞争在市场上非常激烈，因为市场上有很多人才和专家。
    Competition is fierce in the market, saturated with talentsand experts.
    e.g 从网上买的东西的价格比从商场里买的便宜。
    Goods, purchased from the internet, can be cheaper than those from commercial stores.
    Ving/ Ved + n

  2. 对于句子的修饰
    句子，Ving == 句子，which V

  3. n + 形容词短语
    e.g: sth saturated with

妙用形容词短语

  1. 形容词短语后置定语
    e.g: a famous school
    a school famous for its history
  2. 可以和定语从句互换！
    e.g: This is a bag, which is attractive for its design.
    —> This is a bag attractive for its design.

with的用法

  1. with + n (with others)
  2. with + n + adv (with his head down)
  3. with + n + adj (with his eye open)
  4. with + n + 非谓语(with homework to do/ with homework finished)
  5. with + n + 介词短语 (with a book in her hand)

插入语，同位句

  1. 本身可以放在主语前面的结构，插在了主语的后面
    转折：nevertheless, conversely, in contrast, on the contrary, on the other hand
    让步：admittedly
    因果：thus, therefore, as a consequence, as a result
    并列：furthermore, in addition, worse still
    总结：in short, in sum, in conclusion
    观点：in my opinion, in others’ views

    In my opinion, people now … –>

    ​ People, in my opinion, now …

  2. 补充在任何突然想解释说明的部分后面
    托福写作没必要掌握这个，阅读里面用的多一些

同位语（常常用来扣题！）

n1, n2 (n1=n2)

从写作角度来说，用 n2 来扣题

Choosing organic food, a method places environment as the beneficiary, turns out to be achieved by every citizen.

Success, a goal everyone strives for, can be winned by perseverance and diligence.

The advancement in pedagogy, a merit derived from raising questions after class, place instructors as beneficiaries.

Friendship, a significant element in one’s happiness, can be maintained by frequent interaction and communication.

Being willing to accept thoughts from others, therefore, contributes to others’ support and assistance, which is an vital element in facilitating success.

As a result, support and assistance, an vital element in facilitating success, can be acquired by the wilingness to absorb thoughts from others.

Being willing to accept thoughts from others, therefore, people will win the support and help, the crutial element of forcing success.

Moreover, wet rice is cultivated more widely and feeds more of this continent s rural populations than other crop.

With comprehensive ideas coming/ generating/ stemming/ derived from participants, mistaks tends to be decreased, or even eliminated.

inhabiting in cities, on the contrary, ensures the proximity to work, which distracts people from the trouble of commuting for a long distance.

Modern people, admittedly, posses a large quantity of knowledge, but are in the lack of expertise applicable in community activities.

Stuents, therefore, are placed as the victims of current educational system, which is grade-oriented.

Students doing few assignments, in addition, are provided with limited feedback significant to their development and advancement.

Rectifying errors of teachers after class, in summary, places both students and teachers as beneficiaries, due to the reasons mentioned above.

Leaders, in some people’s view, ought to be a proposal arbiter, due to the lack of proficiency/ professional expertise of employees.
